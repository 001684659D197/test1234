Welcome to Seattle!

Seattle, the largest city in Washington State, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich cultural scene and stunning natural surroundings.

Key Statistics:

  • Population: ~769,700
  • Year Incorporated: 1869
  • Region: Puget Sound
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: ~$100,000

Explore iconic landmarks like the Space Needle, indulge in fresh seafood at Pike Place Market, and enjoy the city's thriving arts and technology scenes.
