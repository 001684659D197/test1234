Welcome to Bellevue!

Bellevue, a thriving suburb in the Puget Sound region, offers a perfect blend of urban amenities and scenic beauty.

Key Statistics:

  • Population: ~147,600
  • Year Incorporated: 1953
  • Region: Puget Sound
  • Classification: Suburban
  • Average Income: ~$120,000

Enjoy upscale shopping at Bellevue Square, explore beautiful parks, and experience the city's booming tech industry.

Bellevue: A suburb with a strong economy, cultural attractions, and scenic views of Lake Washington.
